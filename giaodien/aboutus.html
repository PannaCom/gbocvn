About Us

At GolfBooking.Com.Vn, we have a passion for golf. For getting up at 6am for a tee time and for working well after 6pm to make sure everything is right for our golfers, for you. Want to track your game? Relive a shot? Book a round at midnight? We're here. We love golf as much as you do - it's who we are. GolfBooking making it easier for you to play more.

InterGolf

InterGolf, a Golf Tour affiliated company, produces services to power the operations and marketing needs of the golf industry. Founded in 2010 by the Tran Anh Duc and Le Thu Ha., InterGolf service suite is tailored for golf and includes: tee sheet, point of sale, 24/7 reservation center, online booking engines, hotel and resort services, 24/7 support and GolfBooking by the online tee time marketplace connecting millions of golfers with tee times at golf courses worldwide.
